INFLATION & METHODOLOGY

Inflation adjustment: Manhattan Project ($30B) and Apollo Program ($298B) are adjusted to 2024/2025 dollars per the original Reuters sourcing (NPS, Planetary Society). AI, VR/AR, and AV figures are nominal (actual dollars per year, not inflation-adjusted). This is conservative toward historical projects since they get an upward inflation boost while tech figures do not.

Overlap warning: The AI bars use Stanford HAI’s “global corporate AI investment” (Quid data), which already captures AI-related spending in autonomous vehicles, VR/AR, healthcare, etc. The separate VR/AR and AV bars represent total ecosystem investment including non-AI hardware and infrastructure. These categories overlap and should not be summed.

2026 projection: The hatched portion of the bottom bar represents the ~$650B projected for 2026, based on Bridgewater Associates’ estimate (Feb 2026). The solid portion ($2.18T) represents confirmed actuals through 2025 from the Stanford HAI 2026 AI Index Report.
